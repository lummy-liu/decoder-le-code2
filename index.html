IFC职业发展协会

1.Introduction

Suzhou campus Career Development Association of Renmin University of China is a student organization directly under the Suzhou campus of Renmin University of China. The professional development association abides by and practices the practical motto of Renmin University of China, and practices the high-level, distinctive and international school running philosophy of China Law School of Renmin University of China. It is committed to helping the school to cultivate a group of excellent students who are capable, ambitious, responsible, worthy of the excellent educational quality of the school and the high expectations of the society, and to help them adapt to the needs of the human resource market.

2.Our activities

Since its establishment, it has many brand activities, such as "career guidance" career guidance plan for career mentors, large-scale career competition activities of "job to job", series of special guidance of "financial stability", and internal training plan of "professional sea tour". At the same time, it is responsible for school lecture activities, organizing students to visit well-known enterprises, undertaking enterprise propaganda conferences, holding large-scale job fairs, and conducting workplace skills Training and other aspects of the work, fruitful, by the students widely praised.
